I’m a creative developer & teacher from Medellin, Colombia.

I create accessible, creative, award winning websites for individuals and companies. I love to collaborate with agencies and freelancers as well as take on projects by myself.

I teach the next generation of web developers at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences at the associate degree frontend design & development.

From Small studios and Freelancers to campaigns and large international companies, I create websites for all of them.

Sharing knowledge is a big part of what I like about being a developer, from time to time I speak at meetups and in 2024, for the first time, at a conference.
